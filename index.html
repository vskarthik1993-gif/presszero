The hospitality phone problem

In a hotel, the phone is where your highest-value guest still shows up.

The group booking, the suite upgrade, the late arrival, the change of plan — they call. And they call exactly when the front desk is slammed, short-staffed, or asleep. Miss it and they don't leave a message; they book the property next door, or an OTA takes its cut.

◆
